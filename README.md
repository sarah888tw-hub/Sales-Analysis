## Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance using Power BI to identify key business insights such as revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional sales distribution.

## Tools
- Power BI

## Key Insights
- The Technology category generated the highest revenue.
- The West and East regions contribute the most to total sales.
- A small number of products drive a large portion of total revenue. The top product is Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier.
- Sales demand tends to increase toward the end of the year, especially in November.

## Dashboard
The dashboard includes:
- Revenue, Profit, and Orders KPIs
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
